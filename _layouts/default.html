About Me

I'm currently pursuing a PhD in Electrical and Systems Engineering at University of Pennsylvania. My doctoral advisors are Dr. Nikolai Matni and Dr. Vijay Kumar. I graduated from Georgia Tech with a Masters Thesis in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the end of Summer '21. I completed my undergraduate education in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a Minor in Computer Science from NIT Trichy. If you are interested in my research, please take a look at my most recent paper on synthesizing dynamically feasible trajectories by design for applications in underactuated robotics.
